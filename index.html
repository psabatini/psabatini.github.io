About Me

The Professional Behind the Numbers

I am a finance and operations professional with over 15 years of experience across financial operations, customer success operations, project coordination, and accounting. My career has been defined by a rare ability to sit at the intersection of financial precision and operational strategy — translating complex data into decisions that move organizations forward.

At eGain Corporation, I spent nearly a decade building and leading financial reporting systems, overseeing ARR audits, driving CSM commission integrity, and delivering board-level executive intelligence. I became the connective tissue between finance, customer success, and leadership — the person who made sure the numbers told the right story and that the right people acted on them.

I bring advanced Excel skills, NetSuite expertise, and a data-first mindset to every engagement. I thrive in environments where accuracy matters, processes need to be built or fixed, and leadership needs someone they can trust with the numbers that drive strategy.

Currently completing my MBA at Illinois Institute of Technology (GPA 3.52), I am deepening my expertise in business strategy, financial analysis, and organizational leadership — and actively pursuing new opportunities where I can bring senior-level operations expertise to organizations that need it.
